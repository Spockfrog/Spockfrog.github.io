Biography

James Dann is a freelance concept artist and illustrator from Athens, GA. He is a professional artist with 14 years of experience doing digital design work.

He is proficient in ZBrush, Photoshop, Substance Painter, Blender and other industry standard software. His works include digital paintings, 3D sculpting, and illustrations. His realistic work shows great detail and is intricately designed.
